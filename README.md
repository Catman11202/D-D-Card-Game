# 🛡️ D&D-Card-Game: The Grand Blueprint

This repository serves as the development tome for a future D&D-inspired digital card game. No playable builds currently exist; this is the master plan for a tactical experience built within the **Godot Engine**.

---

### 🗺️ The Vision (Development Roadmap)
The goal is to translate the complexity of Dungeons & Dragons into an entertaining, strategic, Godot-powered card game. 

**Future Goals & Planned Mechanics:**
* **Tactical Card Combat:** A planned turn-based system where deck synergy and positioning determine survival.
* **The "Roll" Logic:** Future intent to integrate 1d20-style randomness directly into card effects for a true tabletop feel.
* **Class-Based Decks:** A roadmap feature to include distinct card pools for Fighters, Wizards, and Rogues.
* **Monster AI Archetypes:** Intent to design varying NPC behaviors to simulate classic dungeon crawl encounters.
* **Visual Fidelity:** Planned use of Godot 4.x's 2D/3D engine capabilities for immersive spell effects and UI.
